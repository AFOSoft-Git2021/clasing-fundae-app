Criminal List Vocabulary: 

Accomplice

MEANING:  A person who helps someone else to commit a crime or to do something morally wrong.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • The second mugger was black, taller than his accomplice and stocky wearing a bracelet on his wrist.

  • The offender's accomplice stood at the door holding what appeared to be a shotgun.

  • He can't recall the man's story but clearly it was a pretext for his accomplice to search the house.

  • After the robbery, the men escaped in a stolen car driven by an accomplice.

  • The historical adviser is there not to ensure verisimilitude, but to be an accomplice in furthering the aims of the producer.

Arsonist

MEANING: A person who intentionally starts a fire in order to damage or destroy something, especially a building.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • Police are blaming arsonists for the spate of fires in the Greenfields housing development.

  • Investigators believe an arsonist set the wildfires that burned 20,000 acres of land.

  • The arsonist confessed that he set fire to six businesses.

  • First he began to shy away from the crowd of young street thug arsonist gunslingers.

Assailant

MEANING:  A person who attacks another person.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • None of the witnesses could identify the assailant.

  • Tragedy strikes when Sam is killed by an assailant on a New York street.

  • He described the assailant as a tall man with a big head and bushy hair.

  • The doctoral student was apparently attempting to change a flat tire when his assailant struck.

Assassin

MEANING: Someone ho kills a famous or important person, usually for political reasons or in exchange for money.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • She hired an assassin to eliminate her rival.

  • The assassination could do serious damage to the fragile peace agreement that was signed last month.

  • President Kennedy's assassination had far-reaching repercussions.

  • The assassination was reported in all the newspapers.

  • The book describes the train of events that led up to the assassination.

Bigamist

MEANING: Someone who marries a person while already legally married to someone else.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • The marriage is not valid if one spouse is a bigamist.

  • Five years ago, she discovered her husband was a bigamist.

  • He turns out to be an imposter and a bigamist.

  • There are plenty of bigamists who don't mention previous spouses.

Blackmailer

MEANING: Someone who threatens to tell a harmful secret about another person in order to try to force them to do something.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • Blackmailers threatened Helen with the video they secretly made.

  • They thought he would be easy prey for blackmailers.

  • He turns out to be a blackmailer who is hated and feared by his fellow citizens.

  • I never dreamed that the blackmailers would murder someone to frame me.

Bomber

MEANING: A person who uses bombs.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • Rajiv Gandhi is believed to have been killed by a suicide bomber. 

  • Seventeen people were killed this morning by bombers who targeted a shopping center.

  • Terrorist bombers attacked the hotel, causing devastation and loss of life.

  • Police are searching for a bomber who planted a device under a car in Birmingham last night.

Briber

MEANING: Someone who pays (or otherwise incites) you to commit a wrongful act.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • The briber who confesses actively his bribery before being prosecuted may be given a mitigated punishment or be exempted from punishment.

  • The essence of corruption is the profit-realized process between briber and government officials.

  • Any briber who confesses the bribery voluntarily prior to prosecution may be given a mitigated punishment or be exempted from punishment.

Burglar

MEANING: A person who illegally enters buildings and steals things.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • No household security devices will protect you against the determined burglar.

  • The burglar had left his fingerprints all over the window.

  • The burglars disabled the alarm and used a glass cutter to break into the house.

  • The burglars have been arrested but the jewelry is still missing.

Pedophile

MEANING: Someone who is sexually interested in children. 

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • There have been 107 convictions of pedophile clergy in Australia, but campaigners have insisted that there could be thousands of victims, as only a few cases ever go to court.

  • Mason now faces up to 15 years in prison and a life of being treated as a child molester, though there's no evidence he is a pedophile or has ever interfered with a child in any way.

  • An online predator may be a pedophile writing to a teen from their home or even an inmate from a prison.

  • His pedophile brother Jude hanged himself in his jail cell over the weekend.

  • The CSIs set out to track down a pedophile suspected of murdering an FBI agent's wife and kidnapping his children.

Cyber criminal

MEANING: Are individuals or teams of people who use technology to commit malicious activities on digital systems or networks with the intention of stealing sensitive company information or personal data, and generating profit.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • Cybercriminals may target an individual's private information or corporate data for theft and resale.

  • Some cybercriminals are organized, use advanced techniques and are highly technically skilled. 

Deserter

MEANING: A person who leaves the armed forces without permission.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • Military policemen searched on foot for draft dodgers and deserters.

  • A special committee was set up by the Supreme Council to register deserters in order to try to ensure their legal protection.

  • Matthew was a deserter. He threw down his rifle and ran from the field of battle.

Drug dealer

MEANING: /b> A person who sells illegal drugs.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • He was a small-time drug dealer who carried a .45 caliber pistol and wanted to be like gang members in larger cities.

  • The police are trying to stop the violence by arresting more drug users and street dealers, many of whom, however, are quickly released again.

  • Another suspected drug dealer had more than $1,300 cash on him.

  • Some positive developments in the area are the disappearance of the corner drug dealer and the problem liquor store.

Drunk driver

MEANING : Someone who drives a vehicle after drinking too much alcohol.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • The new law increased prison terms for drunken drivers involved in fatal accidents.

  • The vast majority of alcohol-related accidents involved drunk drivers, he says.

  • I think you have a bigger chance of being hit by a car with a drunk driver without insurance than you do of being killed in a plane crash.

  • We will take a zero-tolerance approach to drunken drivers.

Embezzler

MEANING: 

Someone who secretly takes money that is in their care or that belongs to an organization or business they work for.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • The detectives mistook him for a suspected embezzler.

  • Businesses need to protect themselves from embezzlers.

  • Anthony was a convicted embezzler who later became an executive at a manufacturing company.

  • All six of the embezzlers pleaded guilty and were fined.

  • Embezzlers stole $1.9 million destined for two school districts.

Forger

MEANING: Someone who makes forged copies; a person who makes an illegal copy of something in order to deceive.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • He was the forger of a painting sold as a Rembrandt.

  • The forger swindled the merchants of this city out of large sums of money.

  • If the forger tries to write quickly and naturally he loses accuracy.

  • The forger was in his eighties; however, he had not lost a hair's-breadth of his ability in making swords.

  • Most people are so busy making a living that they forger to make a life.

Gangster

MEANING: A member of an organized group of violent criminals.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • The gangster stole a gun and shot his way out of prison.

  • The story of Romeo and Juliet was reinvented as a Los Angeles gangster movie.

  • He was in no sense a political prisoner, but a gangster and racketeer who rightly did six years in jail.

Hacker

MEANING:   Someone who gets into other people's computer systems without permission in order to find out information or to do something illegal.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • Problems can occur when a company does not know a hacker has broken into its system.

  • When her voicemail inbox became full, hackers reportedly deleted messages to make room for new ones.

  • There are also suggestions that hackers could make expensive phone calls at the owner's expense.

  • Early warnings about hacker attacks and viruses have the potential to save firms trouble and money.

  • The company makes software to protect wireless phones from hackers and viruses.

Hijacker

MEANING: Someone who takes control of an aircraft or other vehicle during a journey by force.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • A hijacker entered her car while it was stopped at a traffic intersection.

  • He acknowledged knowing some hijackers but he denied knowing their plans.

  • The hijacker swung me around in front of him against the police with my body.

  • The hijacker was lifted immediately after he stepped down from the plane.

Hooligan

MEANING: A violent person who fights or causes damage in public places.

SAMPLE SENTENCES: 

  • It is possible that some elements of the Hooligan dress-style were derived from the clothes favored by costermongers in mid-nineteenth-century London.

  • He grabbed the hooligan and shot him out of the door.

  • All this contrasts markedly with the media image of the hooligan as a purely destructive agent.

  • The death-faced hooligan had his arm around her now, comforting her, consoling her.

Industrial spy

MEANING: Refers to the illegal and unethical theft of business trade secrets for use by a competitor to achieve a competitive advantage.

SAMPLE SENTENCE: 

  • Marco is an industrial spy, who steals secrets when his victims are at their most defenseless: when they are asleep, and dreaming.

Kidnapper

MEANING:  Someone who takes a person away illegally by force, usually in order to demand money in exchange for releasing them.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • The alleged kidnapper committed suicide late last night.

  • After collecting the cash, the kidnapper made his escape down the disused railway line.

  • The kidnapper was shot dead then and there by the armed policeman.

  • The police asked for a news embargo while they tried to find the kidnapper.

Looter

MEANING:  Someone, usually one of a large number of people during a violent event, who steals from shops and houses; someone who steals something from a place or person.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • The museum galleries were stripped of artifacts by looters.

  • A mobile looter was arrested by police after he tried to steal a mobile and purse in broad daylight.

  • Mobs of looters went on the rampage in several parts of the city.

  • Pictures of people being arrested and of looters and rioters were shown on TV.

  • The looters took valuables from four locked houses.

Mugger

MEANING: A person who attacks people in order to steal their money.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • A mugger grabbed her handbag as she was walking across the park.

  • We ran after the mugger as fast as we could, but he got away.

  • Late one night a mugger wearing a ski mask jumped into the path of a well-dressed man and stuck a gun in his ribs.

Murderer

MEANING:  The crime of intentionally killing a person.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • The newspaper article included a gruesome description of the murder.

  • He was imprisoned in 1945 for the brutal murder of a twelve-year old girl.

  • He has admitted committing several crimes, including two murders.

  • He died in mysterious circumstances, and there is still a possibility that it was murder.

Offender

MEANING:  A person who is guilty of a crime.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • She is a persistent offender and has been arrested five times this year for shoplifting.

  • Tougher prison sentences may act/serve as a deterrent to other would-be offenders.

  • Here at the special school we make every effort to socialize these young offenders.

  • We're searching for a couple of runaways from the young offenders' institution.

Poacher

MEANING:  One that trespasses or steals. : one who kills or takes wild animals (such as game or fish) illegally.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • Poachers hunt and kill the rhinos for their horns.

  • The rangers' main job is to deter poachers.

  • The poacher beat a hasty retreat when he saw the police coming.

Rapist

MEANING: A person who forces someone to have sex with them.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:

  • The convicted murderer and rapist is scheduled to be executed next Friday.

Rioter

MEANING: One of a group of people who meet in a public place and behave in a noisy, violent, and uncontrolled way, often as a protest.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • She watched rioters setting fire to the vehicles.

  • Soldiers used tear gas to disperse the rioters.

Robber

MEANING:  Someone who steals.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:

Shoplifter

MEANING:  A person who takes goods illegally from a shop without paying for them.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:

Smuggler

MEANING: Someone who takes goods or people into or out of a country illegally.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • An increasing number of migrants are turning to smugglers for help.

  • The bombings were masterminded by a known drug smuggler.

Spy

MEANING: a person employed by a country or organization to secretly gather and report information about another country or organization.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:

Stowaway

MEANING:  a person who hides on a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle without having to pay.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • The crew discovered the stowaway about two days into their voyage.

  • An exception to this solemnity comes when Mr Raban breaks his own rule and allows stowaways on board.

Terrorist

MEANING: someone who uses violent action, or threats of violent action, for political purposes.

SAMPLE ENTENCES:

  • The hostages are being held as a bargaining chip by terrorist organizations.

  • It is thought that they worked in collusion with the terrorist network.

Trespasser

MEANING: a person who goes onto someone's land or enters their building without permission.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:

  • He and his colleagues are constantly frustrated by persistent vandals and trespassers.

  • They're on special lookout for thieves and trespassers.

  • Sophisticated software allows the cameras to detect trespassers in areas off limits to the public.

Vandal

MEANING: a person who intentionally damages property belonging to other people.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:  

  • Vandals smashed windows and overturned cars in the downtown shopping district.

  • The couple set up a 24-hour security camera to trap the vandal scratching their car.

SAMPLE SENTENCES:
