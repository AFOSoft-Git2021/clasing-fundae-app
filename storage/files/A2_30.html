Textures

Texture is the physical feel of something — smooth, rough, fuzzy, slimy, and lots of textures something in between.

Following is a list of adjective words describing texture of food, fabric and skin respectively:

TEXTURE DEFINITION & EXAMPLE EXAMPLE
smooth (of a surface) Even and regular; free from perceptible projections or indentations. silk
downy covered with fine soft hair or feathers. a downy pillow
silky (of a surface) made of silk; resembling silk in texture. dress
velvety (of a surface) smooth and soft to sight or touch; Suggestive of the texture of velvet. jacket
soft (of a surface) Yielding readily to pressure or weight; Smooth or fine to the touch. skin
tender (of a surface) easily broken, cut, or crushed; soft; not tough. steak
slippery (of a surface or object) slippery; Difficult to hold firmly or stand on because it is smooth, wet, or slimy floor when wet
rough (of a surface) not smooth; uneven or irregular. ground
sticky (of a surface) covered or daubed with an adhesive or viscous substance notes
scratchy (of a surface) Having a rough, uncomfortable texture and tending to cause itching or discomfort.
sharp (of an object or surface) Having an edge or point that is able to cut or pierce something knife
bumpy (of a surface) Uneven, with many patches raised above the rest. country road
coarse (of a surface) rough in texture, structure, etc; not fine. Made of large grains or particles. salt
chewy (of food) needing to be chewed hard or for some time before being swallowed. chewing gum
crunchy (of food) firm and making a loud noise when it is eaten crunchy toast
grainy not smooth or fine mustard
flaky tending to form or break apart into thin pieces pie with a crisp
furry covered with fur small furry animals
granulated in small grains granulated sugar
hard solid, firm, and rigid; not easily broken, bent, or pierced. a hard mattress
shiny (of a smooth surface) reflecting light, typically because very clean or polished. shiny hair
fluffly of, like, or covered with fluff. a fluffy towel
sandy covered in or consisting mostly of sand. sandy beach
rubbery having a tough elastic texture, like that of rubber.
wrinkly having many lines or folds. he's old and wrinkly
scaly covered in scales. scaly reptiles
fuzzy having a frizzy, fluffy, or frayed texture or appearance. fuzzy fake-fur throw pillows
