Sentence Connectors to present qualities 

This indicates differences or similarities between arguments, ideas, and situations.

In the same way

Meaning: similarly to

Sample Sentences: 

  •  Make the drink with boiling water in the same way as tea.
  • School meals need to be marketed to children in the same way as other food.
  • You must exercise your mind each day in the same way that you do your body.


Similarly

Meaning: In a similar way

Sample Sentences: 

  • The children were similarly dressed.
  • Cars must stop at red traffic lights: similarly, bicycles have to stop too.
  • Similarly, many of my patients gain weight to protect against stress at home or at work.


On the other hand

Meaning: in a way that is different from the first thing you mentioned

Sample Sentences: 

  • My husband likes classical music – I, on the other hand, like all kinds.
  • I want to go to the party, but on the other hand I ought to be studying.
  • I'd like to eat out, but on the other hand I should be trying to save money.


In contrast

Meaning: If one thing is in contrast to another, it is very different from it.

Sample Sentences: 

  • His public statements have always been in marked contrast to those of his son. 
  • It was cold yesterday, but in contrast it's very hot today.
  • The stock lost 60 cents a share, in contrast to last year, when it gained 21 cents.


Anyway

Meaning: used to give a more important reason for something that you are saying

Sample Sentences: 

  • I don't have time to go, and anyway it's too expensive.
  • The meal isn't nearly ready yet, but I expect that they'll be late anyway.
  • The principal refused to let us try it, and it was a pretty stupid idea anyway.


Likewise

Meaning: In the same way

Sample Sentences: 

  • Just water these plants twice a week, and likewise the ones in the bedroom.
  • We put up a fence, and other neighbors did likewise.
  • The court additionally ruled the newly assigned judge will likewise decide whether other counts are kept together or made separate.


MORE SAMPLE SENTENCES:

  • I was brought up to be polite. Similarly, I have tried to teach my own children good manners.
  • The two books are similarly structured.
  • The teams were similarly equipped.
  • Similarly, Greg's own mother died when he was young.
  • On the other hand, honesty is an absolutely necessary policy as a matter of principle and also credibility.
  •  A Buddhist monk offered prayers for their health and happiness in contrast to the royal couple's general downcast attitude.
  • The sky is lead gray, and so the oranges and yellows of the maples stand out vibrant in contrast.
  • I have no chance of winning the competition, but I'm going to enter anyway.
  • Even if it is pouring with rain, the event will take place anyway.
  • He is our friend and likewise our leader.
  • He duly performed his own half of the bargain and expected the others to do likewise.
  •  He voted for the change and he expected his colleagues to do likewise .
