Materials

These are the elements, constituents, or substances of which something is composed or can be made.

Types of Materials

BUILDING MATERIALS

VOCABULARY MEANING/DEFINITION SAMPLE SENTENCE
glass A hard, brittle substance, typically transparent or translucent, made by fusing sand with soda, lime, and sometimes other ingredients and cooling rapidly. It is used to make windows, drinking containers, and other articles. I'd like to have glass doors.
plastic An artificial substance that can be shaped when soft into many different forms and has many different uses He put a sheet of plastic over the broken window.
wood A hard substance that forms the branches and trunks of trees and can be used as a building material Mahogany is a hard wood and pine is a soft wood.
brick A rectangular block of hard material used for building walls and houses: The chimney was made of bricks.
sand A substance that consists of very small grains of rock, found on beaches and in deserts. The children played all day on the sand.
stone The hard, solid substance found in the ground that is often used for building, or a piece of this They cut enormous blocks of stone out of the hillside.
cement A gray powder that is mixed with water and sand to make mortar or with water, sand, and small stones to make concrete They use cements in building houses.
concrete A very hard building material made by mixing together cement, sand, small stones, and water Don't walk on the concrete until it has set.
gravel Small, rounded stones, often mixed with sand. The workmen shovelled gravel onto the road.
marble A type of very hard rock that has a pattern of lines going through it, feels cold, and can be polished to become smooth and shiny. It was colder than her marble floor!
FABRICS
VOCABULARY MEANING/DEFINITION SAMPLE SENTENCE
cotton The threads that grow around the seeds of a tall plant grown especially in the U.S., China, and South Asia She looked pretty in a simple cotton dress.
cloth Woven or felted fabric made from wool, cotton, or a similar fiber. Lay the cloth flat across the table.
leather Animal skin treated in order to preserve it, and used to make shoes, bags, clothes, equipment, etc Have you seen Chrissie's new leather jacket? It's majorly cool.
lace A decorative cloth made by twisting thin thread in delicate patterns with holes in them A dress trimmed in white lace.
nylon An artificial substance used especially to make clothes, ropes, and brushes. These covers are 100 percent nylon.
polyester An artificial material that is a kind of plastic, often used for making clothes, bags, tents, etc. The backpack is made of hard-wearing polyester.
silk A delicate, soft type of cloth made from a thread produced by silkworms, or the thread itself e was wearing a suit made from pure silk.
wool The soft, thick hair that grows on the bodies of sheep and some other animals Australia is one of the world's main producers of wool.
METALS
VOCABULARY MEANING/DEFINITION SAMPLE SENTENCE
aluminium a chemical element that is a light, silver-coloured metal, used especially for making cooking equipment and aircraft parts Cover the fish with aluminium foil and cook over a low heat.
steel a strong metal that is a mixture of iron and carbon, used for making things that need a strong structure, especially vehicles and buildings Coke is used in the production of steel.
brass a bright yellow metal made from copper and zinc The door handles were made of brass.
bronze a brown metal made of copper and tin The church bells are made of bronze.
copper a chemical element that is a reddish-brown metal, used especially for making wire and coins They mine a lot of copper around these parts.
gold a chemical element that is a valuable, shiny, yellow metal used to make coins and jewelry She wears a gold cross round her neck.
iron a chemical element that is a common grayish-colored metal. It is strong, used in making steel, and exists in very small amounts in blood Iron rusts easily.
platinum a chemical element that is an extremely valuable silver-colored metal, used in jewelry and in industry She is wearing a platinum neclace.
silver a chemical element that is a valuable shiny, white metal, used for making silverware We gave them a dish made of solid silver as a wedding present.
alloy a metal that is made by mixing two or more metals, or a metal and another substance. Brass is an alloy of copper and zinc.
tin a chemical element that is a silver-colored metal, often combined with other metals or used to cover and protect other metals. The country's most valuable commodities include tin and diamonds.
OTHER MATERIALS
VOCABULARY MEANING/DEFINITION SAMPLE SENTENCE
cardboard material like very thick, stiff paper, usually pale brown in color, used especially for making boxes He was living on the streets for three months, and his home was a cardboard box.
rubber an elastic substance made either from the juice of particular tropical trees or artificially These toys are made of pliant rubber, so they won't break.
clay thick, heavy soil that is soft when wet, and hard when dry or baked, used for making bricks and containers Clay is used for making bricks and pots.
mud earth that has been mixed with water olice found tire tracks in the mud.
paper thin, flat material made from crushed wood or cloth, used for writing, printing, or drawing on Dictionaries are usually printed on thin paper.
soil the material on the surface of the ground in which plants grow. Gardening is a way for city dwellers to return to the soil.
