School

School supplies are all the items that students use in school. In other words, these are commodities that a student requires in the course of an academic year.

List of school supplies

VOCABULARY MEANING/DEFINITION SAMPLE SENTENCE
Scissors a device used for cutting materials such as paper, cloth, and hair, consisting of two sharp blades that are joined in the middle, and two handles with holes to put your fingers through. Don’t use these scissors to cut paper or card.
Backpack a bag with shoulder straps that allow it to be carried on one's back. He lay back on the grass using his backpack as a pillow.
Book is a medium for recording information in the form of writing or images, typically composed of many pages bound together and protected by a cover. He laid the book on the bookshelf a moment ago.
Ruler a straight strip or cylinder of plastic, wood, metal, or other rigid material, typically marked at regular intervals, to draw straight lines or measure distances. Can you measure accurately with this ruler?
Pencil a long, thin object, usually made of wood, for writing or drawing, with a sharp black or colored point at one end Write your homework in ink, not in pencil.
Crayons a pencil or stick of colored chalk or wax, used for drawing. These crayons are not suitable to use in very hot weather.
Glue an adhesive substance used for sticking objects or materials together. She mended a broken dish with glue.
Notebook a small book with blank or ruled pages for writing notes in. He brought out a notebook and pen from his pocket.
Paper thin, flat material made from crushed wood or cloth, used for writing, printing, or drawing on Please write in pen on both sides of the paper.
Highlighter a special pen containing bright ink, used to mark words in a book, magazine, etc. Use a highlighter to reinforce the ideas you want to remember.
Calculator a small electronic device that is used for doing calculations I needed to use a calculator to add up the bill.
Pencil sharpener a mechanical, electric, or small handheld device for making a sharp point on pencils I do not have a pencil sharpener, but I have a knife.
Lunch box a container in which to carry a packed meal. I picked up my lunch box and I walked to school.
Paper clip a small piece of bent wire used for holding pieces of paper together She fastened the papers together with a paper clip.
Stapler a device for fastening together sheets of paper with a staple or staples. She stapled the papers together with the stapler.
Pen a long, thin object used for writing or drawing with ink Can I borrow your pen for a minute?
Flashcard a card with a word or picture on it that is used to help students learn Keep the data on flashcards and put it in your pocket to look at in spare moments.
Chalk a stick of this rock or a similar substance used for writing or drawing With a stick of chalk, he wrote her order on a blackboard.

School vocabulary

VOCABULARY MEANING/DEFINITION SAMPLE SENTENCE
teacher someone whose job is to teach in a school or college. My son's never been in trouble with his teachers before.
lessons a period of time in which a person is taught about a subject or how to do something. He gives French lessons.
subjects a branch of knowledge studied or taught in a school, college, or university. Math is favorite subject.
classroom a room, typically in a school, in which a class of students is taught. I don't think another desk will fit into this classroom.
computer an electronic machine that is used for storing, organizing, and finding words, numbers, and pictures, for doing calculations, and for controlling other machines We've put all our records on computer.
desk a type of table that you can work at, often one with drawers She sat at her desk writing letters.
principal the person in charge of a school She was called by the principal.
