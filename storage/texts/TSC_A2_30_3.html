  WHAT IS TEXTURE IN SIMPLE WORDS?               

Texture is the physical feel of something — smooth, rough, fuzzy, slimy, and lots of textures in between. Sandpaper is very rough — it has a gritty, rough texture. Other things, like linoleum, have a smooth texture.

Texture has to do with how an object feels and its ingredients.

WHAT IS TEXTURE WHAT THE 5 WAYS TO DESCRIBE TEXTURE ARE?

Textures can be described as “rough”, “smooth”, “hard”, “soft”, “liquid”, “solid”, “lumpy”, “gritty” etc.

The word “texture” is used for many different things. It can even be used in abstract senses, e.g. for music and poetry.                          

 

WHAT ARE THE TYPES OF TEXTURES?

Some textures include: rough, hard, liquid, solid, wet, bumpy, fuzzy, sticky, dusty, sharp, rough, gritty, soft, lumpy.
