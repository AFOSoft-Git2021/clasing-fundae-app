Waiter: How can I help you sir?

Customer: Could I have a glass of water first, please before I see the menu. 

Waiter: Yes, of course. 

Customer: Could you please tell me the famous dish of your restaurant?

Waiter: Sure! What would you prefer, vegetarian or non vegetarian?

Customer: A non-vegetarian menu, please.

Waiter: Our Sirloin Steak is one of the best dishes we serve.

Customer: Get that one.

Waiter: Anything in beverage?

Customer: Yes, one coke.

Waiter: Very well, sir.

Customer: How long will it take for my order?

Waiter: It will take 20 to 25 minutes.

Customer: Excuse me! I forgot to mention one thing.

Waiter: Yes, please tell me.

Customer: I want coke without ice.

Waiter: Sure, sir.

Customer: While I wait for my meal, could I see the dessert menu?

Waiter: What would you like to have after your meal?

Customer: I would like to have cheesecake after. And also, can I move to the table beside the window, please? 

Waiter: Yeah sure.
