Questions: TC-A1_3_24 to TC-A1_3_27

A little mouse lives in a field. Her name is Holly. She loves to run in grass and wheat. The mouse gathers seeds and grains. Holly has a sack where she puts her grains and seeds. The little mouse counts; one grain, two grains, three grains, four grains, five grains-half of the sack is filled with grains. One seed, two seeds, three seeds, and four seeds- the sack is full. “I’ll take this sack of grains and seeds to my six baby mice,” Holly the mouse says.
