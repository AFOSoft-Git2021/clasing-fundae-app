Planning a visit to the most exciting place in the Middle East?

One of the things to remember while visiting Dubai is that the UAE’s most thrilling city has certain cultural and religious values and rules. 

Here are some things you should know about Dubai:

  • You can wear swimwear on private beaches and swimming pools. Cross-dressing is something you can’t do. 
  • You have to discover the traditions and culture of the city because it can make you feel connected with the area and its people.
  • If you compare traveling via Dubai metro to a taxi then you can see a significant difference in the amount of money you can save, plus you can also save a lot of time by avoiding traffic on the busy roads of the emirate.
  • Tourists generally purchase a silver card or a red ticket for the metro. You can use the silver card for up to 5 years. 
  • If there is anything Dubai has a zero-tolerance for, it is the use of drugs. If you bring in any medication without being aware of the guidelines in Dubai, you could easily get in trouble.
  • No matter how Insta-worthy that photograph is, it is offensive to photograph locals and certain buildings in Dubai, you could get a penalty. 
  • Even a slight trace of alcohol in your system can give you a one-way ticket to prison.
  • Racing, and using your phone while driving can get you fined if caught.
