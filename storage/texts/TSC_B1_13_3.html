Ways to trick yourself into feeling confident, even when you're not.

  • Slumping can make you feel more sad, while sitting up straight and holding your head high can make you feel instantly more powerful.
  • Keeping a positive quote in your mind is always a good idea — it can help you feel good about yourself.
  • Every time you think something bad about yourself, immediately think of something good. You could always find something beautiful about yourself. 
  • Accept that criticism sucks and learn how to move past it.
  • When you're feeling anxious, you can breathe deeply and slowly to calm yourself down.
  • You probably have one thing in mind that you know makes you feel more confident. Maybe it's a dress you think you look great in, or maybe you could wear your favorite lipstick or perfume. 

Again, it's important to remember that confidence is a lifelong journey. It won't always be easy, even if you work really hard at it — and that's okay.
