Max and Alice will go to the countryside this afternoon.

Alice is very excited about the trip. She is going to pack a lot of food.

She will also bring her favorite hats and sunglasses.

Max is going to bring an umbrella in case it rains. He will be the one to drive them to the countryside.

Alice asked Max “Why did you bring an umbrella?”

Max answered, “The sky is getting dark, I think it is going to rain.”

Alice did not agree, “No, the sky will be clear throughout the day.”

“It is better to be ready, it is good if you are going to bring your raincoat.” Max said.

“I don’t want to think it is going to rain, we will be outside, we will play with a lot of animals.” Alice happily replied.

Max replied: “I hope so! Anyway I am going to the gasoline station to fill some gas.”

Alice agreed and smiled at him.
