Different ways to be polite in a conversation.  

  1. Whenever you meet people, always say “hello”.
  1. You have to say please and thank you when you ask something or receive something. 
  1. When someone is talking and you want to say something, you may tell the person “Excuse me, May I say something, please?”
  1. If you are talking to someone nearby, you don’t have to shout when you say something. 
  1. If a person is telling you something, take a moment to acknowledge what they are saying. 
  1. Whenever you are in a conversation, put your phone down, so you can understand what they are telling you. 
  1. When someone asks you something, let them know you’ve heard what they said. 
  1. Don’t be shy to tell them if you can’t hear them well. 
  1. If you don’t agree with something, you may say, “I understand your idea but I think”.
  1.  Always try to say nice things about the person you are talking to.
