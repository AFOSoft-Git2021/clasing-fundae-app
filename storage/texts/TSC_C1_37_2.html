Dear diary,

Today, I staggered while I was trying to learn how to skid on my bike. I wasn’t careful enough and plunged down a steep slope. I sprawled on the dirt before carefully standing up. My body was in tremendous pain. It’s as if someone trampled on it.

My body went limp but I saw a pile of leaves. I had a nice canter around the leaves as if I hadn't taken a fall. I took long, nice strides. I stumbled and fell after a good three minutes of running around. I felt alive. I jumped and bounced off the ground.

I had so much fun that I had to hobble back to my bike. On the way back, I had to wade through the deep piles of leaves. My feet were in so much pain. I decided to walk the way home. I enjoyed the amble in the park.

I’d go and practice my skid as soon as I heal from my injury. I hope I plunge into another fortunately, unfortunate event.

Sincerely,

Matt.
