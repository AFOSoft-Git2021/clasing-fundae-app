Dear Mr. Mills,

I am writing in reply to your complaint about the noise levels coming from the surrounding businesses around the apartment complex.

I don’t want to reinvent the wheel but a few people living on the west wing of the building have expressed concern over the noise levels.

The building committee does not want to beat around the bush. Thus, they have conducted meetings over this issue.

We have talked to the businesses already. However, some of the noises are inevitable due to the nature of their businesses.

We don’t want to cut corners but we want to make sure we provide the right solution.

Out of pocket, we are going to fortify the windows with noise-proof glass.

Once we have finalized the proposals, we will roll up our sleeves in no time.

Until then, we ask for your continued patience.
