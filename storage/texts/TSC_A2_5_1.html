One day, a boy named Dominic decided to plant a magical tree. 

Dominic sat down on the floor. Then, he dug a hole in the ground. 

He planted a seed and the tree grew!

The tree grew so fast, within a day it was already taller than the boy!

Soon, the tree had delicious apples that hung from its branches. 

Dominic wanted to make an apple pie with apples from the tree. 

To get the apples, He shook the tree and apples fell to the ground. 

He picked up the apples off of the ground and took them inside the kitchen. 

There, he cut the apples into tiny pieces and made a pie.
