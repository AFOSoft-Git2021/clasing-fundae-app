Dear diary

I wish to know more about the world. Life is so interesting!

I wish to have more knowledge about the origin of life. I wish to understand the importance of the sun.

The world is so vast. I wish to taste the flavour of every dish. I wish to feel the different textures of food in my mouth. I wish to be able to cook every cuisine known to man.

The life I want sounds like it costs a lot of money. I wish to earn enough. But maybe I wish the world didn't have to use money. So the people would be free to do whatever they want.

I don’t wish for people to be lazy, though. I also don’t wish for the world to be in chaos.

I wish to live in a world where peace and happiness dominate. I wish to make a change. I wish to influence people to want change too.

I wish to grow more as a person. I wish to stay optimistic about things. Last but not least, I wish to stay hungry for knowledge. But of course, too much of anything is bad, so I have to maintain balance.

Sincerely yours,

Luca M.
