Waves around you

Luxury brands are known for exclusivity. They use top-notch materials. The popularity has caused others to try and mimic their products. Bona fide items last a long time.

Despite the unreasonable price, cashmere coats serve its purpose. It is soft and it is a bulwark against the cold. It is still a case of needs vs. wants and whether you can or can’t.

The hoi polloi are the ones easily influenced while the nouveau riche awaits a new release. They are the audience that demands encore.

It’s cliché to wear known brands. There are other brands that also provide the same or even better quality.Don’t be afraid to go to a place that is terra incognita to you.

It might feel like you made a faux pas when you do or wear something that is not for everybody but it is an act of bravado to have a panache personality.
