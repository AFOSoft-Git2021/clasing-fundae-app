Dear Diary,

Last night, as I was sleeping, I had a crazy dream. I was walking down the street by myself at night. 

It was raining hard. Suddenly, I saw a man dressed in black. He was running fast. 

In his arms, he was holding a bag that he had stolen from an old woman. 

The man was carrying a baseball bat in his left hand. 

I was chasing the man for a very long time. 

When I tried to grab the bag, he started swinging the baseball bat but I pushed him to the ground.

I grabbed the bag from the man and took it back to the old woman. 

The woman was crying in fright, but when she saw me return her bag, she smiled and thanked me. 

The next thing I heard was my mom calling my name 

“Alex, Wake up or you’ll be late for school.”
