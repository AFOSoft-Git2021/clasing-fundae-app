A long time ago there was a beautiful princess. 

She lived in a very high tower. A wicked fairy cast a spell and the princess slept for a hundred years. 

A big forest grew around the tower. One day a handsome prince came to the tower. 

He took his sword and cut the trees. The prince fought with the wicked fairy.

He woke the princess with a kiss. 

The prince married the princess and they lived happily ever after.
