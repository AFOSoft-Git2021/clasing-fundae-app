I just figured that my sad feelings are not that bad.

There is no other way to start than to tell you that I am truly blessed. I grew up in a warm, close family that supported me with my dreams. I’ve got friends who love me. My parents afforded me every great opportunity that you can possibly imagine.

I went to summer camp. I traveled all over the country to play hockey.

Despite having it just about as good as a kid could ask, from a young age, I experienced sad feelings that I couldn’t explain myself.

I just figured that my sad feelings are not that bad.

Fast-forward to the start of college. Those feelings were always there, but about to get much worse.

When my parents dropped me off at school, it was the first time that I had to deal with my own emotions.

I didn’t have the chance to cry to my mom like I did before. Like when I came home from high school or I even locked myself in my room after a bad day in school.

This was a new turn in my life that I was not prepared for at all. There wasn’t anybody who could help me out.
