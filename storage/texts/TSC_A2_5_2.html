Nancy takes her daughter Donna to the park. 

The park has lots of trees. It has lots of squirrels and birds. 

The squirrels run up and down the trees. The squirrels chase one another. 

While the birds fly around. 

The squirrels and birds are good neighbors. 

Nancy sits down on a bench. She takes a magazine out of her purse. 

She turns the pages of the magazine.

While Nancy is reading, Donna runs over to a squirrel. She tries to catch the squirrel. 

Donna chases birds. 

After a while, Donna caught one squirrel.
