Dear Eli, 

Thank you for your letter. It was so nice of you to do it. 

Well, I never thought I'd have fun on my first day at school. I know English is not my first language, that's why I had a hard time understanding everyone because they speak so quickly. 

But, I only made some friends. Tom and Ana are such nice people. They like reading, writing, and other such hobbies. They are so good at reading aloud. 

I met one of the nicest teachers at school. Teacher Alis, She is so helpful to me. We had such a good time in her Science class. She can be scary when she gets mad, there were two boys in the class, they were so noisy. I never thought they could do such a thing in class. 

Our school is huge. You should see our library, we have so many books to choose from! 

If you are going to visit the library just bring a jacket, so you won’t catch a cold, it’s freezing there. 

I hope you could be here to try the food in the cafeteria. They have such tasty meals but some of the snacks can be so expensive that I have to bring mine. 

I hope we live close, so you can visit me anytime. Well, time flies so fast, I am sure we’ll see each other soon.

Your loving friend, 

Carol
