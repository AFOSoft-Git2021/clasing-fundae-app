Listof court and law related words

1.>Appeal- An appeal is a request to the public for money, information, or help.

2.Barrister - A barrister is a lawyer entitled to practice as an advocate, particularly in the higher courts.

3.Community service - Community service is work done without payment to help other people. Criminals whose crime was not serious enough for them to be put in prison are sometimes ordered to do community service.

4.Court - A court is a place where trials and other legal cases happen, or the people present in such a place, especially the officials and those deciding if someone is guilty.

5.Court case - A court case is a dispute between two parties that is decided in a court of law.

6.Death penalty – Death penalty is the legal punishment of death for a crime.

7.Fine - A fine is an amount of money that has to be paid as a punishment for not obeying a rule or law.

8.Guilty - Guilty means being responsible for breaking a law.

9.Imprisonment - Imprisonment is the act of putting someone in prison or the condition of being kept in prison.

10.Innocent - Innocent means not guilty of a particular crime.

11.Illegal - Illegal means not allowed by law.

12.Jury - The jury is a group of people who have been chosen to listen to all the facts in a trial in a law court and to decide if a person is guilty or not guilty, or if a claim has been proved.

13.Justice - Justice is the system of laws in a country that judges and punishes people.

14.Offense - An offense is an illegal act.

15.Sentence - A sentence is a punishment given by a judge in court to a person or organization after they have been found guilty of doing something wrong.
