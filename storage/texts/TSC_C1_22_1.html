Things People Regret the Most

1. Time

  • Some people wish they could have given more time to their loved ones.

  • Some people wish they could have established a work-life balance routine.

  • Some people wish they could have started traveling at an early age.

2. Worry less

  • There was research about people wishing they could have thought of asking for help instead of keeping their problems to themselves.

  • There were people saying they could have written the important and not so important things to do.

  • While others believe they could have been optimistic in life.

3. Forgiveness

  • Holding grunge is never healthy, other people believe they could have just forgotten the bad and focus on the good.

  • It can be too late to start wishing you could have forgiven people more.

  • Others say, they could have been strong, they could have learned to let go of the negativity.

4. My life, my rules

  • Spend your time doing what you love and not having to end up saying “I wish I could have followed my passion in life.”

  • It can be difficult to hear a lot of voices dictating what is good for you. Most people regret not following their guts, “I could have listened to my intuition.”, a person once said.

  • Some people believe they could have established a healthy boundary of saying no to other people’s opinion about how to live their lives.

5. Living for the moment

  • You have to make a difference today. Some people realize this sooner in life. “I wish I could have started my own charity, I always wanted to help people in need.”

  • Always ask yourself if there is anything you might regret later in life. There are people who say “I could have known this would happen later on.”

  • If not now, when? If dancing is always what you wanted, chase it and do it. “I wish I could have taken that step.” , don’t wait for the time to say this line.
