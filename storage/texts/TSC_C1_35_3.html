Questions: TSC-C1_35_22 until C1_35_30

Terrorists hijacked a plane

A flight attendant aided and abetted a group of terrorists in hijacking a plane. They kidnapped the passengers and are threatening to murder them unless the government complies with the terrorists. They robbed the passengers and are asking the government to let them escape. One of the terrorists betrayed his group; the terrorists are now arrested, leaving the passengers unharmed.
