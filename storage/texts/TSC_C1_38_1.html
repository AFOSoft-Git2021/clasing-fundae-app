Voice of nature

A prey whimpering in the presence of its predator, a lion letting out a loud roar, a hyena sniggering at its hopeless prey—nature creates sounds.

In the grip of the snake, the bird began to wail with terror. Nature is cruel, but nature is somehow innocent. A monkey guffaws as the other falls down a tree.

The cow stood up and let out a powerful bellow as the clouds that carried the rumble of thunder drew on. The howl of a distant wolf caught the attention of its pack. The chicken cackles as it tries to run away.

A frigid wind surged through the arches, producing a ghoulish whine. The rain hollers as it pours down heavy raindrops. Twigs and sticks snap as animals run for shelter. The unfortunate ones weep in pain as disaster creaks in the distance.

Nature is gentle; it yells. Nature is harsh; it whispers. Nature speaks in different voices.
