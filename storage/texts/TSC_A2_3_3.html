Once upon a time, there was a boy named Alfred who decided to build a new tree house. 

He wanted his tree house to be high.

He walked through his yard and picked up huge pieces of wood that he could find. 

He found some short and long woods and piled them all up near the oak tree. 

He figured he would also need nails and a hammer. 

He went to his father’s workshop and found a hammer that was very old and dirty. 

He also saw some used nails. 

He was on his way to the yard when it started to rain. The floor is slippery to work now.

Alfred started to walk very slow towards his house. 

When he got inside, he put on dry clothes. 

He sat down on his sofa and played a lovely song while he drank his hot tea 

and thought about his tree house.
