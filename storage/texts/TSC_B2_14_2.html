Dear diary,

Today is a terrible day. I lost my money.

I wish I hadn’t forgotten where I put my money. I wish I had spent it on food.

My friend transferred to a different school. I wish we had sung our favorite song before she left. I’ll always treasure our memories. But I wish we had spent more time together.

We had a surprise exam today. I wish I had studied our last lesson. Today was the submission of our assignment. I wish I hadn’t forgotten to do my homework.

I didn’t have the energy to listen to the discussion. I wish I had slept more. I was late for the first time. I wish I hadn’t stayed up late last night.

I saw a guitar that was on sale on the way home. I wish I had found my money. Today is not the best. I wish I had made better decisions.

But that’s life; today might not be great, but who knows what tomorrow brings?

Michael R.
