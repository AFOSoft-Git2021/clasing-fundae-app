Jack and Emma are getting ready to visit the zoo. 

They have to reach there before noon. 

They leave at 10 o'clock and they are going by car. 

The car stops at the traffic signal. 

A pigeon sits on top of the car and Emma says “It looks lovely.”

A puppy runs across the road. The car starts and the pigeon flies away from the car.

There were lots of pigeons flying above the buildings. 

They finally reach the zoo. 

Emma says “Look! The bear is resting under the tree.”

There are a lot of monkeys on the tree. 

Jack saw the peacocks and asked “Can they fly over big buildings?”

Dad says, “No, they can’t.”

There are lions in the zoo. A Lioness is running after the cub. While the crocodiles are resting near the lake. 

Mom takes out the food and everyone enjoys the picnic at the zoo.
