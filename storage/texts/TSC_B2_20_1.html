Questions: TSC-B2_20_1 until B2_20_10

Teacher: Before we end the class, let’s do some exercises. Use “so that” in a sentence.

Zac: It helps to lower blood sugar so that you feel less hungry.

Ken: I stepped aside so that she might come in.

Will: They are specially packaged so that they stack easily.

Mia: We must sink a borehole so that people will have water.

Olivia: The sink shelf is grooved so that the water will run off.

Teacher: Use “in order to” in a sentence.

Zac: I meditate in order to relax.

Ken: Understand yourself in order to better understand others.

Will: He walked about in order to keep warm.

Mia: She skipped lunch in order to go shopping.

Olivia: Teamwork is required in order to achieve these aims.

Teacher: Good job! Class dismissed.
