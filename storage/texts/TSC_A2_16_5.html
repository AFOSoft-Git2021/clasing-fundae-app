FAST FOOD VS VEGETABLE SALAD

Alice: Hi. My name is Alice. I am in a fast-food centre with my father. He is more protective than my mother.  

Father:  You shouldn’t eat a lot of fries. And you should eat more vegetable salad

Alice: why should I do that father?

Father: Because vegetable salad is healthier than fries!

-Alice: But dad, fries are tastier than vegetable salad.

Father: It’s taste is not more important than our health.

Alice: But fries are cheaper than vegetable salad.

Father: Yes, but our health is very expensive. It is more expensive than everything. Nothing is more important than our health. So you should be more careful when you choose your meal.
