Dear Mr. Mills,

I am writing in reply to your complaint about the noise that has been coming from the surrounding businesses around the apartment complex.

A few people living on the west wing of the building have expressed to have been preparing a complaint over the noise levels.

The building committee does not want to beat around the bush. Thus, they have conducted meetings over this issue.

We have been talking to the businesses since the day it happened. However, some of the noises are inevitable due to the nature of their businesses.

We don’t want to cut corners. The best solution is something to be given to all tenants as soon as possible.

Out of pocket, we are going to fortify the windows with noise-proof glass.

Once we have finalized the proposals, the management has said to be bringing in the glasses soon.

Until then, we ask for your continued patience.

Alex Turner

West Wing Complex

Manager
