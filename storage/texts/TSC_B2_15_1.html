Questions: TSC-B2_15_1 until B2_15_14

Lorde: Hey Ruiji, an art museum is up next week. I wish to go there with you.

Ruiji: I wish to see you there. But I might have a tight schedule next week.

Lorde: Why are you busy next week? I wish to speak to your mother and complain.

Ruiji: There’s just a lot going on at school. I didn’t wish to be so busy with school.

Lorde: I’m just joking. I didn’t wish to have a tense conversation with you.

Ruiji: Okay, one of my assignments involves art and I wish to paint somewhere quiet.

Lorde: So where are you going to paint?

Ruiji: I wish to go up the mountains. I also wish someone would appear to help me paint.

Lorde: You know you don’t have to wish for that. I’ll go there with you. So how are we going to go there?

Ruiji: I wish to go there by riding a horse, but I think a bus would be more convenient.

Lorde: Sounds great! I wish to sit in the window seat.

Ruiji: Okay, fine. I wish to drive you there, but I don’t have my licence yet. I also wish to show you where I go when I feel like painting.

Lorde: I didn’t wish for you to bring me there. But I’m so excited!

Ruiji: I’m also excited!
