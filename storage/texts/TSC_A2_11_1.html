Ann is having a birthday party. 

She is six years old today. Ann told her mother she would like a very special cake for her sixth birthday. 

She said she wanted a birthday cake with three layers: A large layer on the bottom, a medium-sized layer in the middle and a small layer on top. 

Her mother went into the kitchen. She is very happy because her mother baked her cake exactly like she wanted.
