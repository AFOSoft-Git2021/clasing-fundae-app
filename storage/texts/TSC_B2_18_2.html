Dear diary 

I want to bring in positive thoughts. I want to look out for opportunities and grab them. I won’t let negativity come in and corrupt my head.

I have to look out for myself. I have to take good care of my body so I don't get burned out. I want to be able to cheer up the people who are close to me.

I want to find out how people found their passion. I want to turn into a passionate person. 

I want my lazy behavior to turn into productive behavior. I want to pay back all the sacrifices my parents made.

There will be a time when I will fall down. When it comes, I want to have the strength to get back up.

I want to make lots of memories. I don’t want to pass away living a boring life. When death comes for me, I want to look back and think of all the moments I shared with my loved ones.

I write lots of silly things here. I hope no one finds out about this diary.

Good night,

Robert.
