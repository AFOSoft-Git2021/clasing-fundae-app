Questions: TSC-B1_15_11 until B1_15_20

One night, three thieves are in a rich man’s house. They will steal some money from him.

The first man will watch the door if someone enters, the second man will put the money in the bag.

The third man got hungry and said "I shall check the fridge for food.”

The first man said 'Don't take too long to check the fridge, maybe the rich man will be back soon.'

Shall we take some jewelry, the second man asked. It will make us richer if we take his expensive watches and rings.

Will those watches and rings be harder to carry? The first man asked.

While the two are busy taking things and stealing money, the man who checked for food wasn't around for a long time.

The first man said "You stay here, I will look for the third man, he is taking so long."

“I took all the things we needed, we shall leave at 6”, said the second man.
