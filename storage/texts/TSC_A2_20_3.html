Questions: TSC-A2_20_21 until A2_20_30

Agent: Skyscanner Travel. How can I help you?

Loe: Yes. I need a flight from London to Bangkok on September 20th. Can you find me the least expensive flight?

Agent: Would you prefer to leave from Gatwick or Heathrow Airport?

Loe: I want to fly out of Gatwick Airport.

Agent: Let me see. Yes. I have a 5:30 flight on Thai Airways.

Loe: 5.30! What’s the check in time? 

Agent: One hour for economy class and thirty minutes for business class. Will you take that?

Loe: No. I won’t get to the airport in time. When will the next flight leave? 

Agent: There won’t be another direct flight on Saturday. There will be one on Friday at the same time.

Loe: I can’t leave before September 20th. What about from Heathrow Airport?

Agent: Just let me check. Oh, there is an 8:30 flight on British Airways.

Loe: How much will that be?

Agent: Economy class costs £386. 

Loe:Alright, let’s go with that. 

Agent: How many seats would you like?

Loe: Just one seat. May I request a window seat, please? 

Agent: Would you like a return ticket?

Loe: No, thanks. When will you send my boarding pass? 

Agent: You will receive the boarding pass after paying. 

Agent: Lastly, the maximum weight per luggage is 23 kg. 

Loe: Thank you!
