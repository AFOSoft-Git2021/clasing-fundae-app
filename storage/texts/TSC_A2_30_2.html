How to Teach Texture!

Texture is fun to teach because students get to use their hands.

That’s right. The more they touch the surface of an object, the better they will understand what that big word – texture – means.

Give them a variety of objects to touch. Maybe let them rub them, stroke them, and even squeeze them.

As your learners touch the surface of different objects, they will notice that things don’t all feel the same.

Some things feel hard, other things feel soft or fluffy, and other things may feel slimy. 

The main point is to get excited with them about all the new ways they can describe objects by the way that they feel.
