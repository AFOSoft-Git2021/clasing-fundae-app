Hi, all. Welcome to the annual Florist Convention.

It’s a pleasure to see so many people here today. My name is Juliette White and I am the president of the Maryland Florist Association.

In a nutshell, this convention will be a great day filled with a lot of informative seminars.

We are also going the extra mile and show practical examples of how to improve your skills in all areas of floristry.

Make sure to have your ducks in a row to not miss anything.

First thing in the morning, everyone should meet at 9:00 A.M. on the street in front of the tulip garden. In the tulip garden, we will showcase the game changer collection of tulips in England.

At the moment, most are blooming so it’s a fantastic opportunity to see how you can make new and exciting arrangements.

To wrap up, I suggest that you visit the aviary before you go home. The combination of the animal life and flowers is truly a sight to behold.
