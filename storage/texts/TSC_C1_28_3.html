Rural Community Outreach Program

What we have is an ongoing commitment to enhance the standard of living in rural communities across the state. The Institute of Rural Service provides annual grants for rural communities.

Not only is our goal to support local efforts to sustain convenience but to satisfy life in the countryside.

The grants available this year are as follows:

Business Development Grant: 250,000 dollars

Community Development Grant: 150,000 dollars

Communications Development Grant: 200,000 dollars

Educational Development Grant: 100,000 dollars

What we will give priority are proposals that reflect a comprehensive approach to community development.

The people who are promoting community engagement will be given assistance.

What we need is to make rural communities a better place to live and work.

What we will allow is all rural communities to take the first step toward a project or plan.

It is the rural community members that will have their lives improved.

The grants were what they used in the past decades for community needs.

Not only as state-of- the-art farming equipment, road maintenance, communications infrastructure, educational facilities, but investments for young rural entrepreneurs.
