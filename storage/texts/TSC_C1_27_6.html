Attention Shoppers

We regret to inform you that there has been a general recall on all Kent brand ladders manufactured between March 30 and July 30 this year.

  • Only once was the ladder stable but most of the time it was not safe and did not comply with mandatory safety standards.

  • Moving while standing on them could contribute to dangerous falls.

  • In no way you will use this ladder for a reason.

Calling or Emailing the Kent Company if you want to receive a refund or a replacement product. Customers will receive a full refund even without proof of purchase as long as they return the ladders.

The Kent Company can be reached by calling 1-800-555-2493.

By pressing 8, you will be transferred to a representative who deals exclusively with product recalls.

We sincerely apologize for this inconvenience.
