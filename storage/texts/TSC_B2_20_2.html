Dear diary,

I keep my door open so that my cat can enter. I light up a mosquito coil so that mosquitoes won’t disturb my sleep. I also put on my insect repellent lotion so that I have double protection. 

 

I adjusted my alarm in order to feed my cat early in the morning. I put my phone near me so that I could hear the alarm.

I tend to forget things. I always put my bag near the door so that I won’t forget it. I live quite far from work. I wake up early in order to not be late. I cook my meals so that I can save money.

I saved up my earnings so that I could buy my own house. Sacrifices are needed in order to achieve your goals.

Yours,

Lorraine.
