Questions: TSC-A2_6_21 until A2_6_30

One lovely evening, Ahmad and his Father went jogging at the park nearby their house. 

Suddenly, Ahmad saw a man lying on the ground. 

Ahmad stopped and shouted at his father to stop jogging. They carried the poor man to the side of the road. 

The man had breathing problems. 

Ahmad quickly called an ambulance. While waiting, Ahmad’s father did CPR to help with the man’s breathing. 

The ambulance arrived after 15 minutes. 

He was sent to hospital immediately. Ahmad and his father continued jogging. 

Ahmad’s father praised him for his quick action.

It was a meaningful day for them because they have done something good.
