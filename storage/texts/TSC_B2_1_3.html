Things to do before and after a workout.

  1. Before you workout, Prioritize getting enough good quality sleep.
  1. Drinking enough water is important before working out.
  1. You should have a pre-snack before hitting the gym!
  1. Before you head out the door, make sure you're wearing the right clothes and footwear for the workout.
  1. Don’t skip your warm-up exercise before the hard workout.
  1. After you workout, make sure you stretch your body out.
  1. Using a foam roller after a workout can help your body to recover.
  1. Not eating after a workout can leave you feeling ravenous later on.
  1. Make sure to keep track of your accomplished routine every after workout you do.
  1. After a workout, consider a cool shower.
