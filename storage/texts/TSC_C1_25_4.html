Subject:Sick Leave

Attention employees,

We are going to make adjustments to our policies concerning sick leave here at Zimnet Corporation.

We are considering in the long run to reduce the number of paid vacation days.

We are planning to increase the number of paid sick days available to employees.

Before we make any changes, we would like to at least gather the opinions of employees on this matter.

We will be calling out employees at random to know your opinions.

Tony Nugent is the person in charge, He will be collecting and analyzing your suggestions.

Afterward, Management will produce a corresponding policy proposal by February 24.

Once the proposal has been made, a meeting will be called to discuss its implementation among all of our employees.

Let’s be participative for a change.

Sincerely,

The management
