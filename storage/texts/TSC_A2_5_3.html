The playground 

It is time for recess. I am on the playground with my friends. First, we play hopscotch. Next, we throw a ball. 

Later, we see the swings, and we race over to them. I pump my swing faster and higher than my friends Jennifer and Peter. 

We get off and run to the slide. We have to wait in line for the slide. When my turn finally comes, I climb to the top of the ladder and sit down on the slide. 

I push off with my hands and go down very fast. 

I laugh because it is fun. Then the bell rings. I quickly drink some water and wave goodbye to Jennifer and Peter.
