Dear Diary,

Tomorrow, I will wake up at five in the morning. I will brush my teeth and will drink a glass of warm water.

Then I will go for a walk. After that I will make breakfast for my family. I will cook lunch and will pack snacks for my husband and daughter.

I will read the newspaper for half an hour and will spend some time with the plants in my garden. After that I will wash clothes while I dust the furniture in the house.

Then I will take a bath and will meditate for 15 minutes. I will eat lunch at one in the afternoon.

My daughter will come home at 1:30. She will complete her homework and will solve math problems. She will then pack her bag for the next day.

My husband will return from the office at 6:30. After coming home he will go for an evening walk.

In the evening, all the children in our neighborhood will meet in the common playing area and they will chit-chat for some time. Then they will play badminton.

We will eat dinner at 7:30. After dinner we will watch television.

We will then read for some time and will go to bed by ten.
