Lulu and the strange noise

It was late in the evening. It was foggy and the streets were empty. 

Lulu was coming back home. She was walking down the shadowy street. 

She wanted to be back home quickly, so she decided to take a short cut.

She didn’t know the place very well. 

She started to walk around the river. Then went over the bushy grass. 

Suddenly, she heard a strange noise behind her. 

She started looking towards the grass… 

When a black cat jumped! 

She fell down on her knees then ran.
