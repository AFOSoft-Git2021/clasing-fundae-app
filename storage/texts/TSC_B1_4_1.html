Bangkok might be noisy and polluted but it's also an exciting city with plenty of things to see and do. 

The Khao San Road was a famous traveller spot because there were a lot of shops.  It was featured in Leonardo di Caprio's film, The Beach. 

For something more authentic, Phra Kanong offers an alternative place to stay, it is where Bangkok people eat and work. It's not as convenient for the main tourist sites, but it has a Skytrain station so you can be at the Grand Palace in 20 minutes.

Bangkok's traffic can be a nightmare. Sure, you can easily take a taxi – if you want to spend hours stuck in traffic jams – but there are two much better ways to get around the city. To explore the temples and historical sites, catch an express boat river taxi.
