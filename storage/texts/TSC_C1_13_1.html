Tech Life Hires New CEO

New York, April 12 — Amid a crisis of low sales and disappointing performance.

Steve Cross is here to be hired by Tech life as its new chief executive officer.

Previously, he was the chief financial officer at the technology investment firm Esta Resources in San Francisco, California.

He assumed the position of CEO at the company—based in Dallas, Texas. He is to be moved this Monday.

Stockholders voted unanimously for the appointment. A renewed idea to be included in the company is what they are hoping he will do as the new CEO.

Mr. Cross has an impressive record as a business leader who makes smart decisions to be shared in the company," commented Lynn Dyer, the director of human resources at Tech Life.

"We have faith that he will lead us in a new direction that will help our markets to be seen worldwide.”

Steve Cross graduated from Chester University in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, with a master's degree in business administration.
