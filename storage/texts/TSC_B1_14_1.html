We are Amanda and Amy, we are twins and best friends. We love to go swimming and do other outdoor activities. 

We may even go to the beach next week. “The weather is supposed to be amazing, Amy said.” 

Amanda shook her head and said “Amy, the weather can change and we might not be able to go after all.”

She pointed at a single cloud outside and waved at it “I hope it goes away, but we may have to find other things to do.” There might be a lot of changes in our activities this summer. 

The girls dream of what they may do if they are able to go to the beach. “Oh, we may build a sand castle, and swim.” Amy said. 

Amanda smiled and replied, “We might see dolphins too!” 

The girls laughed and closed their eyes, imagining their future trip. “We might be able to get ice cream every day, but dad will probably say no.”

“Whatever happens, we will have fun. If it rains, we may be able to go to the arcade.” Amy said. 

Amanda is thinking of other things they might do indoors instead of swimming.
