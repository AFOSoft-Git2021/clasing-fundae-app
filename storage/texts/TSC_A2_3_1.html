Fay and Sophie are good friends. 

They go to the same school and class. 

They are both very intelligent students although they differ in personality. 

Fay is polite and kind to others. 

Whenever she has a problem, she is very calm to solve it.

She gets curious whenever there is a new lesson to learn.

She is respectful to her teachers at school.

Sophie, on the other hand, is very naughty at school. 

She is loud during the lessons. 

Sometimes she can be rude to her classmates. 

She can be very lazy in doing school activities but can be very interested in doing art activities. 

Nevertheless, both are loved and cared for by their teachers.
