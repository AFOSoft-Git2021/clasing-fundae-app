The Heart

The heart is part of your body. The heart is slightly to the left of the center of your chest.

The heart makes the blood move through your body. If you press your ear against someone’s chest you can hear the heart beating.

The heart is very important. To keep it healthy and strong, it is good to eat right and exercise. The heart is also a well-known symbol for love. It is pictured on love letters and valentines.
