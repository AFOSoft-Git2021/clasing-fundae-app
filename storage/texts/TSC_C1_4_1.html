How to make a 55-minute chocolate cake

Ingredients:

You’ll need a 210-gram bag of all-purpose flour.

You’ll need a 400-gram cup of granulated sugar.

You’ll need a 90-gram cup of unsweetened cocoa powder.

You’ll need a teaspoon of baking powder.

You’ll need a teaspoon of kosher salt.

You’ll need a 240-gram cup of buttermilk at room temperature.

You’ll need two large eggs at room temperature.

You’ll need two teaspoons of vanilla extract.

You’ll need a 112-gram cup of vegetable oil.

You’ll need two teaspoons of baking soda.

You’ll need a 237-gram cup of freshly brewed coffee.

Instructions:

  1. Sift the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt into the bowl and mix until combined.

  2. In another bowl, combine a 240-gram cup of buttermilk with oil, eggs, and vanilla.

  3. With the mixer on low speed, slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry. With the mixer still on low, add the coffee and stir just to combine, scraping the bottom of the bowl with a rubber spatula.

  4. Pour the batter into two 8-inch round prepared pans and bake for 30-40 minutes at 350°F, until a cake tester or toothpick comes out mostly clean (not wet).

  5. The pan will need a 10-minute rest, then turn them out onto a cooling rack and cool completely.
