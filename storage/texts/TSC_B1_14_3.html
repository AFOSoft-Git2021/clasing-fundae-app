The Things You Should Do Everyday

  • Be grateful for the things you have- You have to maintain an attitude of gratitude.

You may carry a positive note with you.

 

  • Smile - When you smile, it cheers up other people too. So smile when you see someone, you may never know who may need it.
  • Spend some time doing the things that make you happy- Life shouldn’t be all about working and paying bills. Do something fun, you may start a new hobby.
  • Exercise- Physical activity stimulates the release of dopamine, endorphin, and serotonin – the famous “happy hormones”. You might want to start walking after a meal. 
  • Eat at least one healthy meal- Eating well makes you feel good. You may also cut down your sugar intake. 
  • List down all your goals for the day- Planning your day helps you stay focused and motivated. You don’t really need to sit down to do this. You may write down the notes while you are on the way to work.
  • Drink enough water- Drinking water has lots of health benefits. Plus, your bowel movement may get easier.
  • Spend at least a few minutes outdoors- Go for a walk around the neighborhood or you may walk your dog at the park. 

  • Get enough sleep- When you’re well-rested, you wake up less cranky and with more energy. You might want to put your phone away. 
  • Meditate for a few minutes- You may have 15 minutes of meditation. It is enough to help reduce stress and boost productivity.
