Mary: Hey Linda, do you wish you could fly?

Linda: No, I don’t wish I could fly. Why do you wish you could fly?

Mary: I wish I could fly so that I could travel across the world.

Linda: I also wish I could travel the world. We can travel by plane.

Mary: That costs a lot of money. Now I wish I could be a billionaire.

Linda: That’s cool but do you ever wish you could be a guy?

Mary: No, why would you wish you could be a guy?

Linda: I wish I could be a guy so I could just be topless when I go for a swim.

Mary: Why are we talking about this in the middle of the class? Now I wish I could go home.
