One night a little rabbit was very tired from a very long journey. 

From afar, he noticed something.

“Oh, there’s a wooden chest between the flowers”, he said. 

“It’s a nice place to rest” he thought.

So, the rabbit ran hurriedly towards the chest. 

When he got there, the little rabbit sat on it to rest. 

He looked above the sky and started counting the stars. 

He went off to sleep. 

Suddenly, a loud voice came up! It startled the rabbit. 

It was a very hungry tiger. 

The rabbit jumped. He started to run around the chest. 

After that, he hid under the chest.

He ran beside the chest. 

He hopped in front of the chest. 

Lastly, He even hid behind the chest. 

Suddenly, he thought of a safe place to hide from the hungry tiger!

“I will hide in the chest!”, he thought.
