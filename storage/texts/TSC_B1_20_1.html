My name is Judy. It’s a Saturday afternoon. I am planning a lot of things to do today. I plan to see Debby, she is a friend of mine.

We love playing hide-and-seek. It is a game where one player should cover his or her eyes while other players hide.

If this person sees a player hiding, she must call his name, they have to run in their home base. The player who is left behind will be the one who will cover his eyes for the next round.

We also enjoy playing the hopscotch game. A player has to hop in the first empty hopscotch square. You have to balance yourself on one foot. I always find it difficult to balance mine. But Debby, that body of hers, it is easy for her to do it.

In the hopscotch game, if the player loses his or her balance, they have to return to the first line.

At my age or yours, this is a good game for us to master body control.

The way I balance myself can be different than yours.
