Questions: TSC-A2_21_1 until A2_21_5

Finding the right store

Tourist: Can you recommend a good toy store?

Tour guide: The best toy shop in the area is in the shopping centre.

Tourist: Is there a pharmacy in this area? I would like to buy some medicine.

Tour guide: The nearest pharmacy is a few miles away.

Tourist: Where can I get pet food from?

Tour guide: You can buy pet food in the convenience store. It is on the right corner of this street.

Tourist: Could you direct me to the nearest post office please?

Tour guide: The post office isn’t open on Sundays.

Tourist: Where is the nearest shopping centre located?

Tour guide: There is a shopping centre just around the corner.

Tourist: I would like to buy some gardening tools.

Tour guide: The nearest hardware store is next to the library. I can book a taxi for you.
