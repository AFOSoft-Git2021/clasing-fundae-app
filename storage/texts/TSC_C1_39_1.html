Sunday morning

Ken mumbles in his sleep “it glittered under the spotlight”. He seems to have brought the drab museum with him in his sleep. It’s evident that he savored his night.

His cat caused a clink of dishes in the dining room. The noise woke him up. The tangy drink comforted his lumpy throat. His thirst made the drink palatable.

Hunger made him order a meal. The silverware clinks as he feasts on his meal. His hunger made him savor every bite. The dish emits a pleasant scent. His room reeks of spices. The twittering birds made for an even better ambiance.

A dusty room waiting to be cleaned and the cold breath of silence wrapped his drab morning.
