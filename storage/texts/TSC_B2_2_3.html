Questions: TSC-B2_2_21 until B2_2_30

Eli: Do you know what happened here a long time ago?

Angelo: This apartment was formerly a bakeshop. My dad used to work here in the old days. They baked as people did in ancient times.

Eli: Wow! That’s cool.

Angelo: In the old days, they were the only bakeries around. The owner went bankrupt not long ago. He was forced to sell his property.

Eli: How about your dad? What happened to him?

Angelo: He opened his own bakeshop not long ago. Now he bakes as he did in the old days.

Eli: I’m curious how people baked in ancient times.

Angelo: I’m amazed how something is preserved from a long time ago.
